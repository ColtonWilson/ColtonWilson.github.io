My Story

Web Developer. Wine Lover. Cal Fan.

In college, I got my first taste of coding with Wolfram Mathematica. I found that with all of my classes I worked most voraciously on computer science and could not stop until I found a solution. Unfortunately, my college didn't have a computer science degree, just a couple of classes to take as electives, so I studied for an Applied Mathematics degree while being a member of my school's NCAA Men's Swim Team. Post-graduation, I took on a role in management. I quickly worked my way to the top and became one of the youngest managers on the west coast. However, something was missing, and I wanted to get back into computer science. So I have decided to go back to school and pursue my Master's in Computer Science where I have learned C++ and also take some night classes for HTML, and CSS. I continue to learn more and am looking forward to the next Software Engineering role where I can be a part of a team that is building a product to better the world.
